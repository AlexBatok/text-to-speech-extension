Text to Speech

Read Aloud Any Page

Lightweight Chrome extension that reads any webpage aloud with natural voices. Private, offline, no limits.

Add to Chrome

Why choose this extension

Lightning fast

Under 50 KiB total. Installs in a second, never slows down your browser.

Fully private

Zero data collection. No cloud, no accounts, no tracking. Everything runs locally on your device.

Works offline

Uses your browser's built-in text-to-speech engine. No internet connection needed to read aloud.

Auto language detection

Detects the page language and automatically picks the right voice. Supports every language your system has.

Floating mini-player

A sleek on-page widget with play, pause, skip, and speed controls. Always within reach.

Keyboard shortcuts

Alt+P to play/pause, Alt+S to stop, Alt+. and Alt+, to skip. Hands-free reading.

Built for privacy

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All text processing occurs locally on your device using the browser's built-in text-to-speech engine. No data leaves your browser.

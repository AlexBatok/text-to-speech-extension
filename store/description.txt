Text to Speech — Read Aloud Any Page turns your browser into a powerful text-to-speech reader. Select text or read entire pages aloud with natural-sounding voices — instantly, privately, and without limits.

HIGHLIGHTS

- Reads full pages or selected text aloud with one click
- Works offline — uses your browser's built-in voices, no cloud needed
- Auto-detects page language and picks the matching voice
- Floating mini-player with playback controls on every page
- Keyboard shortcuts for hands-free control (Alt+P, Alt+S, Alt+. Alt+,)
- Highlights the current paragraph as it reads
- Adjustable speed (0.5x–3x), pitch, and volume
- Supports all languages your system voices support
- Dark mode that follows your system theme
- Lightweight — under 50 KiB total

HOW TO USE

1. Navigate to any webpage
2. Click the extension icon or press Alt+P
3. The page starts reading aloud — a floating player appears for quick controls
4. Adjust speed, pitch, and voice from the popup
5. Select specific text and right-click "Read aloud" to read just that section

BUILT FOR PRIVACY

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any data. All text processing happens locally on your device. No account required, no cloud services, no tracking — ever.

CUSTOMIZATION

Choose from all voices installed on your system — Google, Microsoft, and more. Set your preferred speed, pitch, and volume. The extension remembers your settings between sessions.

ACCESSIBILITY

Designed for readers, students, professionals, and anyone who benefits from listening to text. Supports screen readers and follows system color scheme preferences.

KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS

Alt+P — Play / Pause
Alt+S — Stop
Alt+. — Next sentence
Alt+, — Previous sentence

Perfect for articles, documentation, research papers, emails, and any text-heavy webpage.
